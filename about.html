About Spectre

Spectre is an exciting tactical shooter game set in a futuristic world. Players choose from a roster of unique agents, each with special abilities and playstyles.

The game features intense team-based combat across multiple maps. Strategy and teamwork are key to victory.

With a wide variety of weapons and tactical options, every match offers new challenges and opportunities to outplay your opponents.
